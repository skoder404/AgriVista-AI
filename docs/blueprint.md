# **App Name**: गौशाला दर्पण (Gaushala Darpan - Cattle Shed Mirror)

## Core Features:

- Breed Recognition: Identify cattle breeds from images using AI.
- AI-Driven Health Score: Analyze animal images to assess health and provide a health report, acting as a tool for evaluation.
- Market Price Predictor: Estimate market value based on breed, age, and health score.
- Multi-Language Support: Offer the app in multiple regional languages for accessibility.
- Bharat Pashudhan Integration: Enable seamless integration with the government's 'Bharat Pashudhan' app.
- Community Breed Input: Allow users to upload images of new, local breeds to help the AI expand its breed recognition tool.
- Expert Connect: Connect farmers with local vets and breed experts, based on the location and breed of their animal.
- Home Tab: A home tab with all details about the app and its achievements.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Earthy Brown (#A0522D) to evoke the natural and agricultural setting.
- Background color: Light Beige (#F5F5DC), providing a neutral and clean backdrop.
- Accent color: Fresh Green (#90EE90), symbolizing health and growth in agriculture.
- Body and headline font: 'PT Sans' for a modern yet readable feel across languages.
- Use farm-themed icons that are intuitive and easily recognizable.
- Maintain a clean and organized layout, optimizing for ease of navigation.
- Incorporate subtle animations to confirm actions and guide the user.
